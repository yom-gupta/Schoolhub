Key Features

Key Features

Class Monitors and Subject Information

Appointed class monitors can add subject-specific information, class schedules, and homework details, accessible to all students in their respective classes.

Homework Tracker

Students can view and submit assignments through the app and website. Teachers can grade and provide feedback on submitted assignments.

Teacher Attendance

Teachers can mark their attendance through the app, simplifying attendance tracking for school administrators.

School Staff Attendance

School staff can mark their attendance using the app, streamlining the overall attendance management process.

User-Friendly Interface

Enjoy an intuitive and visually appealing app and website design, ensuring a seamless user experience.

News Feed

Stay updated with school activities and events through the news feed feature. Share updates, photos, and videos to foster a sense of community.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can SchoolHub benefit my school?

SchoolHub centralizes school-related information, communication, and management, empowering students, simplifying homework processes, and enhancing overall school engagement.

Can students contribute to subject information and homework tracking?

Yes, SchoolHub allows appointed class monitors to contribute subject-specific information and homework details, accessible to all students in their respective classes.

How does the homework tracker work?

Students can view and submit assignments through the app and website. Teachers can grade and provide feedback on submitted assignments, streamlining the homework submission and grading processes.

Can teachers and school staff mark their attendance?

Absolutely! Teachers and school staff can mark their attendance through the app, making attendance management easier for school administrators.

Is the app and website user-friendly?

Yes, SchoolHub is designed with a user-friendly interface, ensuring an intuitive and visually appealing experience for all users.

How can I stay updated with school activities and events?

SchoolHub's news feed feature allows users to post updates, photos, and videos related to school activities and events, fostering a sense of community and keeping everyone informed.
